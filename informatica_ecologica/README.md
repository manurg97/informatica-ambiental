# Ecological Computing

Ecological computing is an interdisciplinary field that studies the relationships between computing and ecological systems.
It focuses on utilizing technology to support environmental sustainability and conservation efforts. Topics may include:
- Energy-efficient computing solutions
- Green data centers
- Sustainable software engineering practices
- The digital ecology of information systems
- Environmental impacts of computing operations.

The goal is to minimize ecological footprints while enhancing the effectiveness of technology in promoting environmental stewardship.
